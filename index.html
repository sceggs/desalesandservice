Vehicle Servicing

Do you want to make sure that your vehicle is fit and ready for the road for the next 12 months?

I offer various options, from a simple health check to a full service including fluids and filters.

I can even work out a custom service to suit your needs.

MOTs and Preparation

Does your car need an MOT? I can collect you car and arrange the MOT of your car through trusted MOT Station’s in the local area.

Alternatively if you simply want to have a Pre-MOT check done on your car to see if it is going to need any work then I can do this and give you an idea of what outcome you could expect.

Suspension

Have you got some strange noises coming from your suspension?

Knocking, squeaking or rattling over bumps? Lower on one corner that the others?

All of these are things I can investigate and rectify to make your drive more comfortable

Brakes

Brakes squealing or grinding? Don’t feel as good as they used to? or you just want a general check?

All of these are things I can investigate and sort out. Parts are usually available within an hour to get your car back on the road.

Tyres

One thing that a lot of people overlook on their car is their tyres, I can come and check your tyres for their tread depth and pressures to make sure you’re safe on the road.

If you’re in need of new tyres then I can quote for the brand/quality that you need and arrange to have them replaced if needed.

Exhausts

Has your car suddenly got a lot louder? Or maybe you’ve noticed it has broken and fallen down? Failed emissions test on the MOT or you have an emissions light on your dash?

All of these are things i can sort to get you back on the road.

Agricultural

I also specialise in agricultural machinery, covering a wide range of machinery including tractors, tele-handlers, trailers and specialist attachments.

Diagnostics

I have got a variety of the latest diagnostics computers/software for when your car breaks.
If you have got an engine management light on or your car doesn’t seem quite right then I can run a number or checks/tests to diagnose your fault and repair after.
For any light that comes up on your dashboard, I can go in to your cars ECU and find out what is going on.
My software also allows me to perform service/brake resets, calibrations, parameter resets, tell the car it has had a new part fitted and much more.
